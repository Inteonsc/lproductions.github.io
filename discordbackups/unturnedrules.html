Unturned Server Rules:
1. Don't allow your reputation to reach Villian (-200). Anyone who reaches below -200 will lose their inventory privileges, meaning that anything can be taken from them.
2. Don't join a group for malicious reasons (i.e joining to learn of a person's location, the location of their base, etc.) Once you join a group you cannot leave it until 24 hours have passed.
3. There are certain items that cannot be taken from a player. Which player doesn't want what stolen is specified under <put channel here>. You can only protect 5 items.
4. Don't destroy too much of someone's base. Only destroy what you must to gain access.(edited)

Raiding Rules:
- A Raid has to be announced 2 minutes before starting. Note - you cannot be in the base but you can be nearby.
- Each player in the raid can loot up to 50 inventory spaces
-Defenders can loot all unprotected inventory from attackers + 1 item from protected. (this or increased looting).
-Attackers cannot loot Defenders Bodies.
-Defenders cannot take things out of chests or storage areas (lenient- if they weapons are in there they can get it but they cannot take out their valuables etc).
-A raid is finished once either side surrenders or all the enemies have been killed. Note - running from the raid will count as surrendering.
-Once a raid is finished that base cannot be raided for 2 more days.(edited)
